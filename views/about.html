About Me

Inside My World

A sophomore at South China Normal University’s Aberdeen College, majoring in Software Engineering and exploring the intersection of logic, creativity, and human experience.

I’m deeply curious about the world and probably a little too talkative—start a conversation with me, and I’ll happily jump from technology to philosophy to why a perfectly cooked meal can fix everything.

Music is where I find flow.

I love getting lost in rhythms, drumbeats, vocal textures, and the emotions hidden between lyrics. It’s the kind of happiness that makes life feel vivid.

A passionate foodie and traveler, always chasing flavors, stories, and cultures that make the world feel bigger and more beautiful.

Rational in mind, emotional at heart— building with logic, living through feelings. ✨

EMA'S FOOD DIARY

Exploring food feels like collecting little pieces of happiness from different places. Every new flavor is a surprise, every meal tells a story, and every bite brings a kind of joy I can’t explain.

I love wandering through streets, discovering hidden old gems, trying local dishes, and sharing delicious moments with friends. Behind every dish lies the unique food culture of its region, waiting to be discovered.

For me, food is more than just eating—it’s adventure, comfort, and a beautiful way to experience the world.
